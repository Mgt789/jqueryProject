Hornbill Resort

Hornbill Resort is a riverside resort offering cottage style stay in midst of thick forest overlooking River Kali in Ganeshgudi area of Dandeli. The white and mighty river Kali flowing beside is one of the real beauty you would rarely find anywhere else. Hornbill River Resort is located in the serene, lush greenery of the unexplored jungles of Dandeli in northern part of Karnataka state in India. The untamed jungles invite you to explore and embrace the freshness of the nature. The white and mighty river Kali flowing beside is one of the real beauties you would rarely find anywhere else. You can really feel the difference when you wake up to the sweet chirping of the birds and the fresh cool air which gives you a pleasant day ahead. Indulge yourself with white water rafting, bird watching, safari, sanctuary, boating, trekking, rock climbing, rappelling, camping and other fun and adventurous activities. Visit us to get away from the monotonous and unglamorous life of city, to experience wildlife, the vibrant jungle and feel closer to nature. Feel free to choose from tree house, rock house, tents, cottage or clustered cottages.
