Whistling Woods Resorts

Whistling Woodzs Resort is situated at a splendid bend of the river Kali that flows across rocky terrain, making way for some marvelous rapids in the path of the gushing river. The resort enjoys a whopping 400 mtrs of riverfront all to its own. Centered on the thrilling water activities, this eco-resort provides all the luxuries of a hotel so that you can enjoy nature at its best in the most comfortable way. Planning a getaway in Dandeli guarantees a time well spent amidst nature- tranquil luxurious stay, thrilling water sports in the choppy river like white water rafting, boating, kayaking, canoeing, Zip-line, Giant Swing, River Biking and more, lip-smacking food, fun filled experiences of trekking, bird watching, boating, swimming and camp fires. It’s a complete adventure experience for the whole family.
